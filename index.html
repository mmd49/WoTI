What is the Internet

The internet is a series of networks connected by various computers and machines. Over the past few years, these connections have grown exponentially, allowing millions of devices to communicate through wired and wireless transmission.

How does the Internet work

The internet functions by using a series of connected computers worldwide to communicate in a variety of messages. The way these messages can be transmitted can vary from electricity to light and even radio waves. Messages are made of bits, which are simple yes or no signals to the computer. To send much larger files such a word document all the bit is stored in a container called a packet. Larger files will be broken into many packets to send it out across the internet.
