HTTP & HTTPS

HTTP

Hypertext Transfer Protocol

The language used to communicate between web browsers and server.

Through a series of get requests from the server, a web page can be displayed on your device

HTTPS

To ensure that your connection is secure, we use a particular sort of certification to let our device know when our link is legitimate.

This is known as HTTPS (Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure)
