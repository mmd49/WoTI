IP Addresses, Packets, & Routing

IP Address

Internet Protocol Address

Allows a device to locate another device anywhere in the world

We use IP address as an easy, scalable way to find a specific machine that is across nations

Packet

A small collection of electric information known as bits are sent across the internet/

Large media files such as images or songs are broken into smaller packets and sent through the internet and then reconstructed again

Routing

A router is a device used to make sure that all packets sent across the internet follow the cheapest path.

The cheapest path may not always be the most direct one.

The routers make sure that packets will always arrive in quickly

TCP

  • A device that makes sure that all the requested packets are received
  • If any of the packets are missing, the TCP requests the missing packets to the server
