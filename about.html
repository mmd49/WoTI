The History of The Internet An Overview

The main purpose of the World of the Internet Website is to inform viewers of the complex history behind the internet. Here they can learn about its early origins with ARPA Net, the battle for a browser engines and the communities made through the internet such as Facebook.

This website also teaches views the programming and devices used to make sure that the internet runs smoothly such domain name servers, IP addresses, TCPs, and HTML

This website is the final group assignment for IS117 (Website Development course for NJIT). We created this site to demonstrate our web site building skills with the intent of sharing it with colleagues and potential employers.
